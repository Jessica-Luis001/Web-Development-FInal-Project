Residence Hall Association

In an effort to maintain and continuously improve the quality of life on campus, advocates for residents of the Drew campus to the Office of Residential Life and to the greater Drew community; regularly reviews the condition and overall living experience of the Residence Halls; serves as a liaison between Drew University residents and the National Association of College and University Residence Halls; hosts annual events and programs designed to promote community and create an enjoyable atmosphere at Drew University.

What We Offer

Financial Support

Clubs and organizations are able to co-sponsor by requesting for supplies and resources to be purchased.

Resources

Student organizations and clubs alike are able to connect with us about personal issues within the residence halls.

Supplies

Student organizations and clubs are able to reserve a speaker, a project and a movie screen from us.

Volunteers

Our executive board members and general board members are able to help set up or run any event on campus.
